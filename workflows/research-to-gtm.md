# Workflow: Research → GTM

Takes competitive research and turns it into a complete, launch-ready Go-to-Market strategy.

**Estimated time:** 45-90 minutes
**Output:** ICP + positioning + GTM plan + launch checklist

---

## Step 1 — Competitive Analysis (`/competitive`)

Understand the market landscape before defining positioning.

**Input:** List of competitors or product category
**Output:** Competitive matrix + market gaps + battlecards

→ Run `/competitive` and identify:
- Where competitors are strong (avoid head-on competition here)
- Where there are gaps (positioning opportunity)
- What customers are NOT being served well

**Key question to answer before Step 2:**
> "What positioning space is underserved that we can own?"

---

## Step 2 — ICP Definition

Define exactly who we're going after with this launch.

Using insights from Step 1, build the Ideal Customer Profile:

**Beachhead segment:**
> The single most specific customer type we're targeting first — not "SMBs" but "Series A fintech startups in LATAM with 10-50 employees processing cross-border payments"

## Step 3 — GTM Strategy (`/gtm`)

Build the full go-to-market plan using the ICP and positioning from Steps 1-2.

→ Run `/gtm` with the following pre-filled:
- Target segment: ICP from Step 2
- Positioning: gap identified in Step 1
- Competitive context: battlecards from Step 1

---

## Step 4 — Launch Checklist

**T-4 weeks**
- [ ] ICP validated with at least 3 customer conversations
- [ ] Positioning tested with sales/CS team
- [ ] Battlecards distributed
- [ ] Landing page / messaging live for testing

**T-2 weeks**
- [ ] Internal enablement complete (sales, CS, support)
- [ ] Soft launch cohort identified
- [ ] Success metrics baseline captured
- [ ] PR/announcement draft ready

**T-0 (Launch day)**
- [ ] Product live for target segment
- [ ] Announcement published
- [ ] Monitoring dashboard active
- [ ] Escalation path defined

**T+2 weeks (First review)**
- [ ] Funnel metrics reviewed
- [ ] First customer feedback collected
- [ ] Battlecards updated based on real objections
- [ ] Decision: accelerate, adjust, or pause
